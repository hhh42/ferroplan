# Suite — a broad range of problems to map where the engine struggles

26 `rpg-world` problems across flavors and scales (generated by
`../../../benchmarks/scale/gen_suite.py`), run to find the solve/fail boundary.
**22 solve, 4 fail** — and the failures are the interesting part.

## Solves (22) — including surprisingly deep ones

| flavor | problem (scale) | makespan |
|---|---|---|
| marketplace | `trade-stall` (coin 4) | 4 |
| forestry | `grove` 4 · `woodlot` 12 · `forestry` 30 | 7 · 17 · **40** |
| mining | `prospect` 4 · `mineshaft` 14 · `deepmine` 30 | 8 · 28 · **60** |
| smelting | `foundry` 3 · `great-foundry` 8 | 15 · **30** |
| magic | `apothecary` 3 · `grand-alchemy` 10 | 11 · **32** |
| job-shop | `order-2` · `order-3` · `order-5` | 6 · 10 · **11** |
| farming | `smallholding` (1 cycle) | 10 |
| combat | `skirmish` · `patrol` (across a map) | 5 · 17 |
| transport | `freight` (load cargo) | 4 |
| construction | `raise-wall` · `raise-square` | 6 · 11 |
| travel | `maze-6` · `maze-15` · `maze-40` | 3 · 7 · 5 |

Takeaways: **linear accumulation scales deep** — `deepmine` is 15 mining ops
(makespan 60), `forestry`/`great-foundry`/`grand-alchemy` are long single-path
chains and all solve. **Conjunctive "job-shop" orders solve** (even the 5-part
`order-5`) when the parts are short and co-located. **Travel + the reachability
axiom is cheap** at every maze size.

## Fails (4) — where the problems are

| problem | why it fails | the fix |
|---|---|---|
| `trade-market` (coin 12), `trade-bazaar` (coin 30) | **multi-path numeric goal.** coin has *three* sources (`sell-goods`, `mint-coin`, `haul-cargo`); at coin≥12 the search wanders among them. (coin 4 commits to selling and solves.) | a single dominant path, or a smarter heuristic (#45) |
| `farmstead` (grain 6) | **cyclic resource regeneration.** Re-tilling one field over 3 crop cycles toggles `tilled`/`planted`/`irrigated` repeatedly; the search loses the thread. (1 cycle solves.) | more fields in parallel, or one contract per cycle |
| `found-village` | **monolithic multi-structure build** from raw — the known temporal-depth wall (#45). | decompose into per-structure contracts |

So the three failure shapes are: **goals with several interchangeable
achievers**, **cyclic regeneration of the same resource**, and **deep monolithic
multi-part builds**. All three are either decomposition cases or land squarely on
the temporal-heuristic work in #45 — none are correctness bugs.
